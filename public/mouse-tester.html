Mouse Functionality Tester

Provided by TonysTechSupport.com.au

This page helps you manually test your mouse functions. Follow the instructions in each section.

Left Click Test

Click the green button below. If your left mouse button is working, the button should visually react (it will slightly shrink and change color briefly due to CSS active state).

Right Click Test

Right-click anywhere within this section, or specifically on the grey area below. A context menu (a small menu with options like "Back", "Reload", "Inspect", etc.) should appear if your right mouse button is working.

Right-click in this area.

Middle Click (Scroll Wheel Click) Test

Many scroll wheels can also be clicked (this is often called a "middle click"). Try middle-clicking on this link: Test Link (opens Google Australia). If your middle click works, the link should typically open in a new browser tab.

You can also try middle-clicking in this grey area. Some browsers might show a special scroll cursor or have other default behaviors.
